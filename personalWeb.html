Project Title 2: "Enhancing Natural Language Processing with Syntactic Parsing: Uniting CYK (Cocke–Younger–Kasami) Algorithm and Deep Learning Techniques.

This is my proposed research paper submitted to Ivy for consideration. This proposed paper captures the idea of combining the CYK algorithm, a fundamental tool in automata theory for parsing context-free grammars, with advanced deep learning methods in the context of natural language processing. It reflects the potential for cross-disciplinary insights that could lead to significant advancements in both automata study and machine learning research.

Project Title 3: Adversarial Attacks on Machine Learning-based Intrusion Detection Systems

This is another proposed research topic under consideration. This proposed topic, attempts to explore how adversarial attacks can be used to bypass machine learning-based intrusion detection systems (IDS). Develop and test novel techniques to enhance the robustness of IDS against adversarial examples, ultimately leading to more secure detection of network intrusions.
